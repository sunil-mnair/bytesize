Sunil Nair - Author and Educator

Sunil Nair is an Author, Educator and Data Science Professional. His career began in 2002 in Dubai within the IT training sector. He then moved to the corporate world in 2003 taking up IT as a profession. He moved from a management position to eventually co-founding a training company in 2019.

Academically, Sunil holds a Master of Marketing with MAHE, Manipal University; and a Post Graduate Diploma in Data Science and Business Analytics from the McCombs School of Business, University of Texas, Austin.
